Becca's Favorite Classic Movies

Top 3

Sound of music

This movie follows a single father and his children as they try to figure out living with a new nanny. They are also dealing with Nazi Germany taking over Austria's government.

Wizard of oz

A girl and her dog get lost in the land Oz. She meets some wonderful people, the Witch of the East, Tin Man, Lion, and Scarecrow while fending off the wicked Witch of the west and trying to find her way home to Kansas.

White Christmas

Old war buddies take their show on the road and run into some old friends and make some new ones.
